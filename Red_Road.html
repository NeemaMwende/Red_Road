Circular Economy

We provide free education in the rural countryside while developing sustainable solutions for waste and poverty. We are creating opportunities for social enterprises that are ecologically & environmentally viable.

Accepting Volunteers

We have volunteers and interns worldwide that work with us to develop sustainability programs that create a social and educational environment where all who are involved are able to collectively thrive. We are currently developing a digital skill set program and a fully self-sustainable eco-village as a scalable model for eco-construction.
